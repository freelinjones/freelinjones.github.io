"Education is the key to open the great door of freedom."

The modern academic library is the perfect place to ply my passion for access to digital information. UT Chattanooga is the perfect place to start my career, because it is evident that the UTC Library, by hiring a Web Services Librarian, is committed to expanding upon its physical collection by utilizing digital tools. This position at the UTC Library combines all of the best parts of my information career up to this point and puts me in an academic library, someplace I had never before dreamt of working but now cannot see myself anywhere else. My background in journalism gave me a passion for research, and turning raw data into consumable content. My experience in marketing communications helped fan the flames of my interest in digital content delivery systems.

I am a self-starter, self-confident, well-spoken, energetic, confident, and personable--the kind of person on whom your students and faculty will come to rely. I come to the library from a different path than most of my colleagues. However, I come with a unique and fresh perspective on information.

If you have further questions, please feel free to contact me.
